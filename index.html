Research Excellence

SPSU Udaipur is creating an enabling environment for a sustainable industry-academia partnership. We seek to bridge the gap between what industry needs and the readiness of graduates when they enter the workforce. Our students gain exposure to industry-relevant knowledge and competencies, and learn life-lessons from mentors.
